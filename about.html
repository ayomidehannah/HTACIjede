About HOLY TRINITY ANGLICAN CHURCH
IJEDE, IKOROUDU ,LAGOS STATE

Christ For All Nations

Our Vision

To be the leading Diocese in the Church of Nigeria in preparing this Nation for the second coming of our Lord Jesus Christ.

Our Mission
  • To ensure a sustained growth in the number of churches and worshippers within the Diocese.
  • To continue to enhance the mode of worship, teach and preach the word of God in all our churches thereby creating worshippers fit for the kingdom of God.
  • To assist every member in the understanding and practice of the 39 Articles of our faith.
  • To develop and inspire in every Church the principle of self-sufficiency and Christian generosity, thereby ensuring a strong and viable Diocese.
  • To continue to promote peaceful and cordial relationship with other Dioceses in order to ensure the success of our common commitment.

Bisi Olaleye Anglican Nursery and Primary School

Bisi Olaleye Anglican Nursery and Primary School, Ijede, is dedicated to providing quality education rooted in Christian values. Our school offers a nurturing environment where children grow academically, spiritually, and morally.

  • Nursery, Primary, and Junior Secondary education
  • Qualified and experienced teachers
  • Modern classrooms and learning facilities
  • Emphasis on moral and spiritual development
  • Active participation in sports and cultural activities

We welcome all children regardless of background, and strive to build future leaders with strong character and faith.
